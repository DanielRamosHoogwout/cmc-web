About me

Melani Costa

After a lifetime dedicated to elite sport, I know what a body can give — and what it needs to recover. That first-hand experience is the foundation of this centre: a place where physiotherapy, rehabilitation and exercise work together.

My goal is for everyone who walks through the door, athlete or not, to receive the same care and the same commitment: a tailored plan with real follow-up, until you reach your goal.

"Change begins with a single step."
